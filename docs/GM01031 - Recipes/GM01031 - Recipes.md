## Deserts

### Rice Crispy Squares

##### Ingredients

- Rice Crispy,

- Mars mellows,

- Butter (approx. 100g),

- Chocolate (optional)

##### Method

1)  In a Wok/Large Pan, fry to butter until golden brown. Best to use a
    medium heat.

2)  Add in the march mellows and stir until a thick liquid,

3)  Add in the rice crispy and mix together. Add more until satisfied
    with the consistency.

4)  Meanwhile, dice the chocolate into chips.

5)  Turn off the heat, and add the mixture to a bowl piece-by-piece
    slowly integrating the chocolate chips so the chips are spread out
    more throughout the mixture.

If the mixture is too hot, the chips will melt.

6)  In a square tin, place some baking paper (does not have to be exact
    nor held down with butter). You need this else the squares will be a
    nightmare to get out.

7)  Pour the mixture into the tin, spread our the mixture evenly then
    compress by placing a wooden chopping board on top and standing on
    it (make sure not to break either the tin, board, or your neck.

Make sure to slice the rice crispy square now!

> If you wait until the mixture has cooled in the fridge, everything
> will crumble apart like your life. If you are using a deep tin, make
> thinner slices.

### Pancake Spaghetti

##### Ingredients

- All purpose flour (2 cups)

- Baking powder (2 tbsp),

- Sugar (1 tbsp),

- Oreos (approx. 10),

- Milk (1 ¼ cups),

- Egg (1),

- Vanilla extract (2 tbsp),

- Melted Butter (3 tbsp).

##### Method

1)  Add flour, baking powder, and sugar to a bowl and mix.

2)  Crush up the oreos into a blender and add to the mixture. You might
    want to leave some crushed oreos as a garnish.

3)  In another bowl, combine the milk, egg, and vanilla extract and beat
    until fully incorporated.

4)  Slowly whisk in the melted butter to the milk.

5)  Combine the wet ingredients with the dry ingredients and mix until
    fully incorporated then add mixture to a squeeze bottle. An old
    ketchup bottle will do.

> Don't wash the bottle in hot water or the dish washer as the clear
> bottle will shrink whilst the lid will remain the same size causing a
> leak.

6)  Pre-heat a pan on medium heat then squeeze out the mixture into a
    spiral shape. Cover and leave for approx. 3 minutes.

7)  Use tongs to collect the pancake spaghetti and set aside ready to
    eat.

## Side Dishes

### Egg Fried Rice

##### Ingredients

- Rice (pre-cooked),

- Eggs (about 4),

- Spring Onions,

- Soy Sauce

##### Method

1)  Dice the spring onions and any extra add-ins.

2)  Get your Wok, heat it up and add in the oil.

3)  Pour in the eggs and scramble until fluffy but not dry.

4)  Add in some more oil then add in any meats then wait for the Wok to
    get back up to heat before you add anything else in.

5)  Add in your spring onions and other veg and then add in your
    pre-cooked rice.

6)  Pour in the soy sauce around the rim of the Wok then mix.

## Mains

### Carbonara

##### Ingredients

- Pasta,

- Bacon,

- Eggs (approx. 4),

- Parmesan cheese

##### Method

1)  Dice up your bacon in cubes.

2)  Fry the bacon in a pan.

3)  Whilst frying, boil in pasta in a pan with pre-boiled water. SALT!

4)  In a bowl, take the four egg yolks (the yellow bits) and some grated
    cheese and mix. Add in some black pepper as well. Should look like
    the yolk of a boiled eggs if mixed properly.

5)  Once you bacon is fried, add in a ladle of pasta water from the
    pasta pan (duh) and mix.

6)  Add your pasta to the pan of bacon and mix.

7)  Once mixed, add you bacon and pasta to the bowl of eggs and mix. The
    heat from the bacon and pasta should cause the eggs and cheese to
    melt creating the famous carbonara sauce.

### Chicken and Chorizo Rigatoni

##### Ingredients

- - Chorizo,

- Chicken,

- Onions,

- Double crème (150ml),

- Pasta,

- Paprika,

- Garlic,

- Salt,

- Pepper,

- Tomato Puree (1 tbsp),

- Basil leaves,

- Parmesan cheese.

##### Method

1)  Chop come chorizo into about 1cm cubes and cry in a pan with some
    oil, diced onions, and some garlic.

2)  Once the onions are a nice gold colour, add in the chopped chicken.

3)  Once the chicken as whitened, add in the Paprika, Basil, Pepper,
    Salt, and garlic.

4)  Once the spices have been mixed in, add in about 1 tbsp.

5)  Once incorporated, add in the double crème and simmer for 5 minutes

6)  During this, boil your pasta with salt.

7)  Add your cooked pasta to the sauce and mix.

8)  Finally, top with basil leaves and parmesan cheese.

### Creamy Mushroom Pasta

##### Ingredients

- Mushrooms,

- Pasta,

- Chicken Breasts,

- Onions,

- Garlic,

- Knob of butter

##### Method

1)  Slice in the chicken in half length ways and spice up both sides
    with your choice of spices.

2)  Fry the chicken in a pan with oil.

3)  Meanwhile, dice up some onions.

4)  Once the chicken is cooked, take out of the pan and leave to rest
    then add in the onions and butter to the pan.

5)  Add the mushrooms and fry then add the garlic and thyme. Add pepper
    and garlic powder midway through frying.

6)  Add in your double crème.

7)  Whilst all this is happening, you should've boiled some pasta. Once
    the pasta is ready, add it to the pan with some mozzarella and mix.

8)  Once mixed, add the pasta, sauce, and chicken to a plate.

## Lunches

### Fancy Cheese On Toast

##### Ingredients

- - Tiger Bread,

- Mozzarella,

- Avocado,

- Salt,

- Pepper,

- Salami

- Green Pesto,

- BBQ Sauce,

- Tomatoes,

- Lemon juice,

- Butter,

- garlic

##### Method

1)  Slice the Tiger bread in half length ways and coat with butter and
    garlic.

2)  Mashup some avocado, lemon juice, salt, and pepper and spread onto
    the bread.

3)  Place a generous amount of salami slices onto the bread.

4)  Place a generous amount of mozzarella slices on top of the salami.

5)  Slice up some tomatoes and place them on top.

6)  Bake in the oven at 180\* from about 10 minutes.

7)  Take out and squirt some BBQ sauce on top.
